HERSHEY'S Classic Milk Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Description

Classics are classics for a reason, and HERSHEY’S Classic Milk Chocolate Chip Cookies simply cannot be beaten in the world of baked goods. It doesn’t matter if the batch is being served to a class full of buzzing kids, a large family with lots of different dessert preferences or a room packed with businesspeople mid-conference. With a plate of warm, home-cooked chocolate chip cookies dotted with soft, melty HERSHEY’S milk chocolate chips, you can bet that all 60 treats in the batch will disappear in no time. Classic!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (2 sticks), softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups HERSHEY'S Milk Chocolate Chips (11.5 oz pkg.)
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla in large bowl with mixer until creamy. Add eggs; beat well. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually add to butter mixture, beating until well blended. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts, if desired. Drop by teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  3. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely. Makes about 5 dozen cookies.
